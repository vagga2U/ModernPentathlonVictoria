Will Barber is an experienced Accredited Exercise Scientist and
Strength and Conditioning Coach based at Gen Health Hamilton. With
a profound dedication to optimizing athletic performance, and a track
record of helping runners achieve remarkable results, Will has become
a sought-after coach for runners of all ages and abilities.
State swimmer and now sought-after coach Paige Clay has extensive
experience coaching young swimmers at all levels, developing skills to
lead swimmers on her team to State and National levels. Originally
from Portland, Paige is currently based in Geelong, where she works
as a school teacher whilst is training for a 100km Ultra Marathon.
An internationally qualified Modern Pentathlon judge in all phases of the
sport, Kate Thompson has been invaluable as technical director at
Australian National events. Her passion for this sport has led Kate to
drive the Australian pony club tetrathlon movement, creating pathways to
Modern Pentathlon. Kate serves as State tetrathlon coach for Pony Club
Victoria and is vigorously involved at a national and international level.
